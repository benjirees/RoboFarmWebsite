Current Active Robots

(Hover)

This is the farming drone - The farming drone will be used to autonomously water crops and apply insecticide when necessary. massively cutting down on human labour and costs.

This is the farm rover - The farm rover will travel across the farmlands and check the fertility of the soil. if the soil isn't fertile it will fertilise it.

This is the food supplier - The food supplier delivers food to the animals on the farm, it will get deployed at certain times throughout the day and make sure the animals are fed.

The Crop Preserver - This is the crop preserver, it will analyse the crops and make sure they are still alive and healthy. It is equipped with sensors.

The Robot Rocket - This is will cut the grass on the farm, measuring the grass height and cutting it if necessary.

The Spider - The spider robot is a crop analyser used for rough and rocky terrain, the advanced suspension allows it to travel over uneven and bumpy land.

GUI - This is an image of the control interface that will be used to control the robots.

Waterer - The watering robot does just that. Waters the crops when they need to be watered.
