About Us

At farmbotics we strive to bridge the gap between farming and technology. We are a relatively small company with large aspirations, within the next decade we strongly believe farming could be 90% autonomous.

We are at the forefront of a technological revolution that will change the farming industry forever. We have a range of robots that independently carry out farming tasks that usually have to be performed by humans.

Join us on this journey in making the farming industry a more sustainable industry for everyone.
